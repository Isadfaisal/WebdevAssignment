Part 7 prologue

In September 1890, jockeys from around the world compete in the Steel Ball Run,
a cross-country horse race from San Diego to New York City, offering a grand
prize of fifty million dollars. Johnny Joestar, a paraplegic, enters the race to
learn about the mysterious Spin ability from Gyro Zeppeli, a former Neapolitan
executioner who temporarily restored his mobility. Although initially rivals,
Johnny and Gyro form a bond as they face numerous dangers from assassins,
outlaws, and violent competitors. The race, organized by oil tycoon Steven Steel,
is secretly manipulated by U.S. President Funny Valentine to collect pieces of
the Holy Corpse, believed to be the body of Jesus Christ.
