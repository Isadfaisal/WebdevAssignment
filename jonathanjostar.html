Part 1 Prologue

England, a young Jonathan Joestar (known to his friends as JoJo)
peacefully lives at his father George Joestar I's wealthy estate.
Another young boy from London, Dio Brando,
is adopted by them due to his father Dario Brando's death.
George believed that he and his infant son were rescued by Dario
during a stagecoach accident in which his wife lost her life;
in reality, Dario was attempting to loot their corpses.
Jonathan attempts to befriend Dio, unaware
of his plan to break his spirit through constant
bullying and earn George's trust so that he can
become the sole heir to the Joestar fortune.
