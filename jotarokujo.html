Stand: Star Platinum

It is a humanoid close range stand
his stand use radius is only 5 meters
but in terms of strength. It is the strongest
and most precise, out of the other stands,
Even accurate enough to perform surgery.

Part 3 prologue

The story is set long after the events of Battle Tendency and follows
Joseph Joestar's grandson Jotaro Kujo, a Japanese high school
delinquent who awakens Star Platinum, a guardian spirit
known as a Stand with superhuman abilities. Alongside his grandfather
and other Stand-users, they are tasked to go on a journey to Cairo,
Egypt, in order to defeat Dio, who is revealed to be alive and is
seeking revenge on the Joestar family.
