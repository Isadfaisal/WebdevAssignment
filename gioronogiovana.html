Part 5 prologue

Golden Wind takes place in 2001, two years after Diamond is Unbreakable,
with Koichi Hirose traveling to Italy to find Giorno Giovanna,
the 15-year-old son of Dio Brando. Giorno, possessing the Joestar
bloodline,he wields the Stand Gold experiance, After a series of
encounters, Giorno joins the mafia group Passione with the
goal of taking it over and ending its illegal activities. He allies
with Bruno Bucciarati and the group embarks on a mission to protect
the boss's daughter, Trish, while facing threats from the organization.
